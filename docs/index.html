How it works

Three steps. One verdict.

No spreadsheets. No manual calculations. No guessing. PARSIO does the heavy lifting — you make the call.

01
📄

Upload the statement

PDF or Excel. Encrypted or plain. PARSIO detects the bank, the layout, and the format automatically — no template setup required.

02
⚡

PARSIO reads it

Every transaction is extracted, classified, and verified. Credits, debits, self-transfers, reversals, recycled income — all handled with precision.

03
✦

Receive the decision

Turnover, DTI, maximum loan, repayment schedule, interest rate — a complete credit picture with a clear approved or declined verdict.

Capabilities

Built for the precision this work demands.

Every feature exists because a real loan officer needed it. Nothing is added for show.

🏦

Any bank, any format

Fidelity, Zenith, GTBank, Moniepoint, OPay, Jaiz and more. Bank and layout detected automatically, with extracted totals verified against the statement's own figures.

🛡

Fraud & recycling detection

Round-tripped funds, self-deposits, and recycled income are isolated and excluded. Blacklisted counterparties are flagged before money moves.

🔍

Smart credit classification

Loan disbursals, reversals, non-business income, and savings round-trips are separated from genuine business turnover — automatically.

📊

Debit intelligence

Existing loan repayments, rent, utilities, and bank charges surfaced for officer visibility — the obligations that shape repayment capacity.

🧮

Full loan computation

Eligible income, DTI, maximum principal, tenor, interest, and a complete repayment schedule — generated in one pass.

📋

Audit-ready reports

Every figure traces back to the underlying transactions. Export a complete analysis your credit committee can stand behind.
